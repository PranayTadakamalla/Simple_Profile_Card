Profile Card

Nikola Tesla

Serbian-American Engineer, Futurist, and Inventor

“The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine.”

Born: July 10, 1856

Died: January 7, 1943

Notable Inventions:
Alternating Current (AC)
Induction Motor
Tesla Coil
Wireless Communication

Achievements:
Established the foundation for modern electrical engineering.
Contributed to the development of radio technology.
Developed the concept of wireless energy transmission.
